Top down design example - Walking a dog!

1 Find your dog
  1.1 Get up from where you are
  1.2 Walk throughout house
  1.3 Look through each room
  1.4 Call dog's name
2 Ask dog if they want to go for a walk
3 Put leash on dog
  3.1 Find the leash
  3.2 Pick up the leash
  3.3 Tell dog to sit
  3.4 Grab dog's collar
  3.5 Grab leashes hook
  3.6 Hook the leash to the dogs collar
4 Put on shoes
5 Open door
  5.1 Unlock door
  5.2 Grab handle
  5.3 Turn handle
6 Take dog outside
7 Walk dog outside
